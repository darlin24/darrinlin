Graduates

Graduates from the class of 2022. A color scheme of black/gold to continue the yearbook theme presented on the cover. The clouds of the lower left/right corners are carryovers from prom designs from me, creating a common theme among the events celebrating the end of their high school lives. The offset rectangular frame above each senior is to create unity between the picture and the quote box below it. The moon icon beside the title and the clouds on the bottom are all hand-drawn.
